# LeadSens User Guide

## Getting Started

### 1. Sign Up
Create your account using Google OAuth or email/password. Your 14-day free trial starts immediately — no credit card required.

### 2. Connect Gmail
Go to **Settings** and click "Connect Google Account." This lets LeadSens:
- Import your email history (up to 2 years)
- Automatically capture every email sent/received
- Sync your calendar meetings
- Use your Gmail for outbound sequences

### 3. Define Your ICP
Open the **Chat** and describe your ideal customer:
> "I sell developer tools to Series A-C SaaS companies with 50-500 employees in the US"

LeadSens will build your TAM (Total Addressable Market) automatically.

### 4. Review Your Pipeline
Check **Accounts** to see auto-discovered companies, scored and ranked by fit. Click any account to see:
- Company details (enriched from real data)
- Suggested contacts
- Activity timeline
- AI-generated insights

### 5. Start Outbound
Go to **Sequences** to create your first outbound campaign:
1. Click "New Sequence"
2. Select target contacts
3. Let AI generate personalized emails for each step
4. Review and approve emails
5. Activate the sequence

## Core Features

### Chat
Ask your CRM anything in natural language:
- "Show me deals closing this month"
- "What did Jane from Acme say about pricing?"
- "Which contacts haven't replied in 2 weeks?"
- "Draft a follow-up email to John at TechCorp"

Every answer includes citations — click to see the source email, meeting, or record.

### Accounts
Company records enriched with real data:
- Industry, size, revenue, tech stack
- ML-based fit score with explanations
- Signal detection (job postings, funding, tech changes)
- Full activity timeline

### Contacts
People you're talking to:
- Auto-created from email interactions
- Enriched with title, LinkedIn, department
- Engagement scoring
- Activity timeline

### Opportunities
Your deal pipeline:
- Kanban board with customizable stages
- Deal coaching based on real pipeline data
- Risk detection and proactive alerts
- Win/loss analytics

### Sequences
Multi-step outbound campaigns:
- AI-generated, personalized emails
- Automatic mailbox rotation
- Reply detection and auto-stop
- Deliverability monitoring

### Deliverability
Monitor email health:
- Bounce rates by mailbox and domain
- Warm-up progress
- Health scores
- Send volume tracking

### Tasks
Track follow-ups and action items:
- Auto-generated from AI suggestions
- Due date tracking
- Priority levels

### Meetings
Calendar integration:
- Synced from Google Calendar
- Linked to contacts and accounts
- Meeting notes and summaries

## FAQ

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're GDPR-compliant. You can export or delete all your data at any time from Settings.

**Q: Can I use my own email domain?**
A: Yes. Connect any Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 mailbox. We recommend using a dedicated sending domain for outbound.

**Q: How does the AI work?**
A: LeadSens uses Claude (by Anthropic) for reasoning and chat, and OpenAI for semantic search. Your data is never used to train AI models.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes. Cancel from Settings > Billing. Your data is preserved for 30 days after cancellation.

**Q: What happens when my trial ends?**
A: You'll be prompted to choose a paid plan. Your data is preserved — you just can't access premium features until you subscribe.
