# LeadSens Demo Video Script

**Duration**: 2 minutes
**Format**: Screen recording with voiceover
**Audience**: Early-stage founders doing founder-led sales

---

## Shot List

### Opening (0:00 - 0:10)
**Visual**: LeadSens landing page
**VO**: "Meet LeadSens — the autonomous GTM engine built for founders who sell."

### Sign Up (0:10 - 0:20)
**Visual**: Click "Start Free Trial" → Google OAuth → Dashboard appears
**VO**: "Sign up in seconds. Connect your Gmail. LeadSens immediately starts importing your email history and building your pipeline."

### Chat-First CRM (0:20 - 0:40)
**Visual**: Open Chat → Type "Show me my top accounts this week" → AI responds with ranked list, citations
**VO**: "Ask your CRM anything. LeadSens answers with real data and links to the source — every email, every meeting, every interaction."

### Auto-Built TAM (0:40 - 1:00)
**Visual**: Chat → "Find SaaS companies in NYC with 50-200 employees using React" → TAM builds in real-time → Accounts page shows scored results
**VO**: "Describe your ideal customer. LeadSens builds your Total Addressable Market automatically — scored, ranked, and enriched with real data from company databases."

### Account Deep Dive (1:00 - 1:15)
**Visual**: Click into an account → Show enriched details, score reasons, signals, suggested contacts
**VO**: "Every account is enriched with industry data, tech stack, funding signals, and ML scoring. Plus AI-suggested contacts with the right titles at every company."

### Outbound Sequences (1:15 - 1:35)
**Visual**: Sequences page → Create new → AI generates personalized emails → Review and approve → Activate
**VO**: "Create outbound sequences in minutes. AI writes personalized emails for each contact based on their company data and your previous interactions. Review, approve, and launch."

### Deal Coaching (1:35 - 1:50)
**Visual**: Pipeline view → Click a deal → AI coaching panel shows specific insights
**VO**: "As deals progress, LeadSens coaches you with specific, data-driven insights. Not generic advice — real analysis of your conversations, your pipeline velocity, your win patterns."

### Closing (1:50 - 2:00)
**Visual**: Dashboard overview → Zoom out to landing page
**VO**: "LeadSens. Your entire GTM engine, on autopilot. Start your free trial at elevay.dev."

---

## Production Notes

- Record at 1440px width, dark theme
- Use real data (Martin's test account with real companies)
- Highlight the speed of AI responses (use fast cuts)
- Background music: subtle, techy, upbeat
- No face cam needed — screen recording + VO is sufficient
- Export at 1080p, upload to landing page + YouTube
